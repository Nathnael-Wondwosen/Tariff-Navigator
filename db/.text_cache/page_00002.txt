INTRODUCTION

This publication contains the official text of the Explanatory Notes to the Harmonized
Commodity Description and Coding System (Harmonized System), including the text of the
Subheading Explanatory Notes indicating the scope and content of certain of the Harmonized
System subheadings (*).

Where a Subheading Explanatory Note is provided this is indicated by the sign (+) after the text of
the heading.

The Explanatory Notes to the Harmonized System are published in English and French, the two
official languages of the World Customs Organization. They are kept up-to-date by the issue of new
pages to replace those affected by the amendments made.

(*) A heading number placed between square brackets indicates that the corresponding heading
and Explanatory Note have been deleted (example : heading [25.27]).
